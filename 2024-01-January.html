Membership
It's that special time of year again when it is time to pay your annual lodge dues!

The lodge currently has close to 2000 members, but of those members only half are in good standing which means that they have not paid their dues for the 2024 year.

The biggest reason to pay dues is to remain an active member of the lodge. As an active member, you will have the opportunity to become a Brotherhood or Vigil Honor member, help with official service projects, receive our newsletter “Unami Update” on a monthly basis, run for official lodge or chapter positions, seek leadership opportunities within the Lodge, and much more. Unami Lodge also uses dues to pay for various camp improvements and support other Cradle of Liberty Council programs. All of the projects and programs supported by the lodge are identified and voted upon by the Lodge Executive Committee.

The Early bird price that expires on January 31 for adults (ages 21 and over) is $25, and youth (20 and under) is only $20.00 which is not even $2.00 a month, a pretty good deal when you think about it. After January 31, the price of dues for adults will be $28 and youth will be $25.

Still haven't paid your dues yet? It's simple, go to UnamiLodge.org/dues and click on the “Pay My Dues” button in the middle of the screen.

Lastly, Unami Lodge, One has introduced the member Portal. Where you can update your personal information, learn about upcoming lodge events and contact other lodge members. If you do not have access or think you do but do not know, visit UnamiLodge.org/dues for more information.

Events
Unami Lodge, One will be hosting our annual weekends again this year, so follow along and update your calendar!
  • Spring Service One will be held on April 5-7 at Musser Scout Reservation.
  • Spring Service Two will be held on May 17-19 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation.
  • We also are having a Summer Inductions weekend again this year! Summer Inductions will be held on August 9-11 at Musser Scout Reservation.
  • Fall Fellowship will be held on October 4-6 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation.
  • The events committee will also be helping out with the Brotherhood Blitz and will be planning our 109th Annual Lodge Dinner.
Whether you are undergoing the ordeal, sealing the bond of brotherhood, or are a general attending member, everyone is welcome to attend all of the weekends.

Every service weekend includes fellowship, service, and obviously; fun! Our theme that has been selected for this year is based on music genres! We will be discovering rock, disco, pop, country, and hip hop music over our 5 weekends, and tying the year together ending with the Grammy Awards!

Keep your eye on the Unami Lodge Calendar for these dates and locations and even more events! If you don't want to register for the weekends individually, purchase the turtle pass. It's a one stop shop for registration and payment!

Please note that you MUST be a dues-paying member to attend UNLESS you are taking your ordeal. We can't wait to rock out with you at our events!

National Order of the Arrow Conference (NOAC)

There's still time to register for NOAC 2024 with the Unami Contingent! The National Order of the Arrow Conference, or NOAC for short, is the premier OA experience. Every two years, over 8,000 Arrowmen from across the country descend on a university campus to come together for a week of fellowship, training, and absolutely unforgettable experiences. NOAC 2024 will be hosted this summer at the University of Colorado Boulder July 29th to August 3rd. Spots are limited and registration closes January 31st, so visit our website UnamiLodge.org/noac to register today!

Also be sure to check out these new Unami Lodge LEC Quarter-Zips! These new properties are a NOAC fundraiser and purchasing them will not only give you a stylish new lodge jacket, but will also go towards off-setting the costs for NOAC this summer.

Lastly, the NOAC Committee is excited to announce that we will be hosting a Merit Badge College this spring for all scouts! The event will be hosted at Archbishop Wood High School in Warminster, PA, on February 24th. Registration is available here: https://scoutingevent.com/525-80827 . Registration is open to all scouts, so be sure to tell your friends, families, districts, and units!
