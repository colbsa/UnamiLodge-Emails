Events
Thank you to all those who joined us for Spring Service One! This was our biggest service weekend in over a decade, all because of our amazing members and candidates who came out for an amazing time! We had over 265 people in attendance, we welcomed 100 new Ordeal Members and 40 Brothers Sealed their Bond of Brotherhood! Unami Lodge, One will be hosting our annual Spring Service 2 weekend at Resica Falls Scout Reservation. Spring Service Two will be held on May 19-21 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation.

Whether you are undergoing the ordeal, sealing the bond of brotherhood, or are a general attending member, everyone is welcome to attend both weekends. With every service weekend comes fellowship, service, and obviously; fun! Our theme that has been selected for this year is based on the 4 elements. With a strong emphasis on choosing your own adventure, each member will receive a choose-your-own-adventure passport that will carry over throughout the weekends. Please note that you MUST be a dues-paying member to attend UNLESS you are taking your ordeal.

Conclave

Dear Unami Lodge, One,

What are you doing June 9 -11th 2023? We know what we are doing! We are going to CONCLAVE!!! Conclave is a special weekend where all the lodges in our section meet for fun, fellowship, and the chance to take home the coveted Conclave Cup!! The conclave cup is the culmination of scouting spirit, and competitions throughout the weekend. This year the competition will include games such as volleyball, and tug-o-war! As you all may know, Conclave will be held at Citta Scout Reservation this year, and sign-ups are now open!! Please go to the Section E17 website to sign up or click the big red banner below!!

With Great Anticipation,
Unami Lodge Conclave Committee
