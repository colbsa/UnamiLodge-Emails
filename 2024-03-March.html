Lodge Operations
Do you know any arrowmen that deserve recognition for the extraordinary work they have done for the Order of the Arrow and Scouting? Nominate them TODAY!

The 2024 Unami Lodge, One award nominations are officially open! Nominating an arrowmen for an award is a great and easy way to show your appreciation for their hard efforts to improve the Order and Scouting as a whole.

Founder's Award: Recognizes Arrowmen who have given outstanding service to their lodge. The award is reserved for an Arrowman who demonstrates that he or she personifies the spirit of selfless service, as advocated by founder E. Urner Goodman and cofounder Carroll A. Edson.

Thomas J. Richard's Outstanding Service Award: Presented to those youth members (under 21 years old) of Unami Lodge, One who serve the Order of the Arrow and their fellow Arrowmen to a high degree.

David Fortunato Outstanding Service Award: Presented to those adult members (over 21 years old) of Unami Lodge, One who serve the Order of the Arrow and their fellow Arrowmen to a high degree.

Charles J. “Chuck” Benshetler Spirit of Camp Leadership Award: Presented to arrowmen (who need not be a member of Unami Lodge, One) in recognition of outstanding service provided as a paid or volunteer member of the Cradle of Liberty Council summer camp staff.

Green Turtle Award: recognizes individuals, businesses, or corporations who are not members of the Order of the Arrow, but have still greatly contributed to the success of Unami Lodge, One.

History
Greetings Unami Lodge, One!

The History Committee is doing really well thanks to all of your support! We are excited to see everyone at the upcoming lodge weekends and set up our pop-up museum. We have two things to announce.

First, we are launching a quarterly newsletter to keep folks up to date on what we are working on and our achievements. To subscribe to our newsletter please visit https://lp.constantcontactpages.com/su/qrw8jmk.

The second thing is we are having our Memorabilia Show at Emmanuel Lutheran Church in Pottstown on Friday, August 23rd and Saturday, August 24th. We will also be hosting two merit badges (Collections and Scouting Heritage) at the show for free but registration is required. For more info or to register for the merit badge classes please visit UnamiLodge.org/news/2024-Scout-Memorabilia-Show.
