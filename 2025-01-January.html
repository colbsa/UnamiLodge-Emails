Events
Pack your bags and get ready for a year of adventure and fellowship as we explore the globe with fellow Arrowmen. Mark your calendars and join us for this unforgettable journey:
  • Spring Service Weekend 1: April 4-6 at Musser Scout Reservation — Step into the Netherlands with tulips, windmills, and Dutch-inspired fun!
  • Spring Service Weekend 2: May 16-18 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation — G'day, mates! Dive into an Australia theme full of Outback excitement.
  • Summer Inductions: August 1-3 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation — Celebrate with the rhythm and vibrancy of Brazil's Amazon Rainforest.
  • Fall Fellowship: October 3-5 at Musser Scout Reservation — Experience the beauty of Japan with its harmony, traditions, and fellowship.
  • 106th Lodge Dinner: January 3, 2026 — Cap it off with a delicious Italian-themed celebration!

Membership
It's that special time of year again, it is time to pay your annual lodge dues! By paying your dues, you can remain an active member of Unami Lodge, One. This means you can become a Brotherhood or Vigil Honor member, help with official service projects, receive the Unami Update Newsletter, seek lodge leadership opportunities, and much more.

- Still need to pay your dues? It's simple, go to UnamiLodge.org/dues and click on the “Pay My Dues” button in the middle of the screen.

- The Early bird price that expires on January 31 is $25 for adults (ages 21 and over), and $20 for youth (20 and under). That’s not even $2.00 a month! It's a pretty good deal when you think about it. After January 31st, the price of dues will increase to $28 for adults and $25 for youth.

- Lastly, Unami Lodge, One has introduced the member Portal. Where you can update your personal information, learn about upcoming lodge events, and contact other lodge members. If you do not have access or think you do but do not know, visit UnamiLodge.org/dues for more information.

- If you have any inquiries about membership in our amazing lodge, please contact the membership committee by email membership@unamilodge.org.

Conclave
This summer, August 6th-10th, members of Unami Lodge will be traveling to Guajataka Scout Reservation in Puerto Rico for Section E17’s Conclave!! The best way for us to keep our prices down is through the help of our fundraiser items. Our fundraiser items include a patch set ($20) and a dri-fit long sleeve ($30) which you can purchase at weekends and on our online trading post! If you are interested in attending this year's conclave, we highly recommend you sign up and join our waitlist. Also for all of you who are attending, there will be a virtual informational session coming in late April / early May, so keep your eyes peeled for that! As always if you have any questions please reach out to the Conclave Committee at ,conclave@unamilodge.org!

The Conclave fundraisers are now available on the Unami Lodge Trading Post.
