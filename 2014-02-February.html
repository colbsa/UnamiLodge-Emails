Vigil Nominations

The Vigil Honor is the highest honor a lodge can bestow on one of its members. It is an honor reserved for those who go above and beyond what they are called to do in the lodge, camp, council or all of scouting. If that sounds like anyone you know, make sure you submit a Vigil Nomination Form at UnamiLodge.org. This form can be completed and submitted online but make sure to complete it as soon as possible; nominations are due on the last day of this month!
 
Conclave

This year's Section NE-5B Conclave will be hosted by Lowwapaneu Lodge 191 at Goose Pond Scout Reservation on the weekend of June 6-8. The theme for this year is "Journey to Trail's End". This year's conclave will have activities such as open COPE, climbing, boating and swimming. Also, sports competitions, ceremonies competitions, shooting sports, and a surprise keynote event! Be sure to mark your calendars for this exciting event of fun, fellowship, and training! For always up to date information and to register for this year's Conclave, visit us at UnamiLodge.org.
 
Membership Cards
Over the past month the Membership Committee has received over 1200 dues payments and has been busy updating lodge records with dues payments and any new personal information we have received . Unami Lodge expected to mail the 2014 membership cards by the middle of February but due to the weather and power issues in the area we expect the new membership cards to be mailed within the next 2 weeks. If you have not yet submitted your dues, feel free to do so on our Due's Page at UnamiLodge.org where you can also always find up to date information about this. Thanks to all those who helped update our records this past month!
 
Community Service Day

Unami Lodge's ninth annual Community Service Day is coming March 29th. This day will run from 8:30 a.m. until 1:00 p.m. and will consist of general upkeep of the land so don't forget your work gloves! This event is free and of course water will be provided. All we ask is that you bring a lunch and your cheerful service! There are currently three locations planned across the council to offer the easiest accessibility. This is one of the lodge's events that is not restricted to arrowmen so feel free to bring the troop to give us a hand. Feel free to check out our article at UnamiLodge.org to get more information about the day and locations which will kept up to date.
