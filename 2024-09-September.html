Lodge Operations
Do you know any arrowmen that deserve recognition for the extraordinary work they have done for the Order of the Arrow and Scouting? Nominate them TODAY! The 2024 Unami Lodge, One award nominations are officially open! Nominating an Arrowmen for an award is a great and easy way to show your appreciation for their hard efforts to improve the Order and Scouting as a whole.

Founder's Award: Recognizes Arrowmen who have given outstanding service to their lodge. The award is reserved for an Arrowman who demonstrates that he or she personifies the spirit of selfless service, as advocated by founder E. Urner Goodman and cofounder Carroll A. Edson.

Thomas J. Richard's Outstanding Service Award: Presented to those youth members (under 21 years old) of Unami Lodge, One who serve the Order of the Arrow and their fellow Arrowmen to a high degree. David Fortunato Outstanding Service Award: Presented to those adult members (over 21 years old) of Unami Lodge, One who serve the Order of the Arrow and their fellow Arrowmen to a high degree.

Charles J. “Chuck” Benshetler Spirit of Camp Leadership Award: Presented to Arrowmen (who need not be a member of Unami Lodge, One) in recognition of outstanding service provided as a paid or volunteer member of the Cradle of Liberty Council summer camp staff.

Green Turtle Award: Recognizes individuals, businesses, or corporations who are not members of the Order of the Arrow, but have still greatly contributed to the success of Unami Lodge, One.

Submit a nomination here: UnamiLodge.org/awards

Camp Promotions
Hello brothers, I know everyone is very sad about summer camp being over but that does not mean the camp fun is also over. Coming up this fall are many activities at both Musser and Resica. On October 19th, 20th, 26th, 27th, and November 2nd, 3rd Cub Scout fall fest is going to be held at Musser. At Cub Scout Fall Fest, cubs will spend the day doing fun activities such as Pumpkin carving, Archery, fishing, orienteering, and much more! Also coming up on October 26th is Halloweekend at Resica Falls! Scouts and their families can participate in many fun activities such as pumpkin painting, Haunted hayride, haunted lodge, and best of all Nudge’s Haunted Kitchen! Sign up while you still can!

Brotherhood
Seal your Bond of Brotherhood at the Brotherhood Blitz! Different from a normal service weekend where you camp out, the Blitz is a ONE DAY event where you show up Saturday morning and walk the Brotherhood Trail. You then do some service and seal your Bond of Brotherhood, all in time to be home for dinner the same day. The Brotherhood Committee hopes to see you there!
