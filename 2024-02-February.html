Events
Unami Lodge, One will be hosting our annual weekends again this year, so follow along and update your calendar!
  • Spring Service One will be held on April 5-7 at Musser Scout Reservation.
  • Spring Service Two will be held on May 17-19 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation.
  • We also are having a Summer Inductions weekend again this year! Summer Inductions will be held on August 9-11 at Musser Scout Reservation.
  • Fall Fellowship will be held on October 4-6 at Resica Falls Scout Reservation.
  • The events committee will also be helping out with the Brotherhood Blitz and will be planning our 109th Annual Lodge Dinner.
Whether you are undergoing the ordeal, sealing the bond of brotherhood, or are a general attending member, everyone is welcome to attend all of the weekends.

Every service weekend includes fellowship, service, and obviously; fun! Our theme that has been selected for this year is based on music genres! We will be discovering rock, disco, pop, country, and hip hop music over our 5 weekends, and tying the year together ending with the Grammy Awards!

Keep your eye on the Unami Lodge Calendar for these dates and locations and even more events! If you don't want to register for the weekends individually, purchase the turtle pass. It's a one stop shop for registration and payment!

Please note that you MUST be a dues-paying member to attend UNLESS you are taking your ordeal. We can't wait to rock out with you at our events!

Weekend and Turtle Pass Registration: scoutingevent.com/525-68803
