The Chief's Message

I would simply like to start off the year by congratulating all new officers, chairmen, and chapter chiefs for taking on or being inducted into their new positions. There is a long year ahead for all of us, but I have confidence that every person on the Lodge Executive Committee is more than capable at performing in his respective position. Now, while the LEC may have some of the most skilled scouts that scouting has to offer, there is always something new to be learned. It is for this reason that I urge all LEC members to make it to the Lodge Leadership Development day. For all members of the LEC (especially newer members), it is essential that you make it to the LLD at 8:30 on February 9th at the Valley Forge Council Office.I cannot stress the importance enough that all LEC members make it to this training day. As the only official day of training the lodge

holds, this is the only real opportunity you have to get an understanding of how the LEC works. The LLD will also be a good opportunity to pick up and hone new skills and ideas. This day of training will provide an excellent chance for LEC members to get to know each other and will allow for those members who are unfamiliar with other members to have a chance to work together. If you have not marked the date for the LLD on your calendar I urge you to do so. Also, if you were not planning on attending the LLD, I am here to tell you that you probably made a mistake and forgot that you were actually going. On a more serious note please do everything in your power to attempt to attend the LLD.

WWW,
Ben Lambert
