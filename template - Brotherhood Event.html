Seal the Bond of Brotherhood!
Congratulations! According to our records, it has been over 10 months since you took your Ordeal. This means that you are now eligible to seal the Bond of Brotherhood. Becoming a Brotherhood member in the Order will complete your understanding of what it means to be a brother in Unami Lodge, One.
The upcoming Brotherhood Blitz is an event at which elegibile brothers can seal the bond of brotherhood. The event will be held on Saturday, December 7th at Camp Delmont's Nelson Training Center. Here are a couple things to remember about this event.
1.
Arrive and check-in at The Nelson Training Center located at Camp Delmont between 8:30 and 9:00 am in work clothes. Program will begin promptly at 9 am. The event will end at approximately 5:30 pm.
2.
Bring work gloves AND class A uniform with ordeal sash.
3.
Program consists of service project, brotherhood trail, and brotherhood ceremony, all in one day!
4.
Cost is $30.00 and includes lunch, all necessary materials, and brotherhood sash.
5.
Questions about the processes or why you are receiving this email can be answered by emailing brotherhood@unamilodge.org .
